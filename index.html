Welcome to the K-fit website.


We are a MMA and Submission Wrestling Club based in Athenry. We primarily train during the day to help facilitate people working shift work, hospitality sector etc. Our Head Coach is Galway native Kevin Moran. Kevin has 12 years experience in MMA primarily but has also trained and competed in many other combat sports such as Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Kickboxing and Sambo to name a few. Training sessions are geared for everyone, be it a complete beginner and all the way to a seasoned grappler. The emphasis in training sessions will be on sports specific movements, partner drills and live grappling. Our start and end times are open ended on both sides to help people training on lunch breaks or other time constraints.

MMA

Mixed martial arts (MMA) sometimes referred to as cage fighting, is a full-contact combat sport based on striking, grappling and ground fighting, incorporating techniques from various combat sports and martial arts from around the world.
